What we do, Our Services

We under-take all types of civil constructions, specialised in building homes, with a 25 years experience in the construction field, having undertaken all types of constructions. We are in the pioneer construction field in sri lanka, covering design and planning.

We focus mainly on moden homes design and constructions, which are purley based on modern technology and cover all aspects, pertaining to engineering, masonry, carpentry, steel and aluminum, m & e work and in addition, we also under-take building hotels, steal ware-houses, cabanas, factory buildings, and swimming pools.
